About ClinIQ RAG

ClinIQ uses Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) with embedded MOH Clinical Practice Guidelines to ground AI responses in evidence-based medicine. Instead of relying purely on generative AI (which can hallucinate), ClinIQ retrieves the relevant CPG section and injects it into the prompt context — so every recommendation cites MOH guidelines.
