📚 Step 1 — Ground the AI on a Clinical Guideline
Anchoring the AI to an authoritative reference reduces hallucination and keeps suggestions aligned with Malaysian practice. Choose a CPG, or upload the actual guideline / formulary PDF for stronger grounding. This is optional but strongly recommended.
🧭 Retrieval-grounding (RAG)

Two ways to ground: Select a Malaysian MOH CPG — anchors the model to a named, authoritative guideline. Upload a PDF / text — injects the document's actual content as context (the strongest anti-hallucination option for dosing & criteria).

Can't find it? Browse the full list at MaHTAS CPG library, or query AskCPG. For best accuracy, download the PDF and upload it under "Upload Reference Doc".
⚠Reference document — not patient data. The first ~7,000 characters are passed to the AI as grounding context. Use guidelines, protocols, or formularies. PDFs are read as text in-browser; for scanned/image PDFs, paste text instead.
