This tool is under active development. All AI-generated outputs must be reviewed, corrected, and signed off by a qualified clinician. Human-in-the-Loop (HITL) verification is mandatory before any output is used clinically.

It is advisable not to enter patient-identifiable information such as full name or IC number. Audio files are processed locally and deleted immediately after transcription. Feedback submitted may be anonymised.

This tool does not constitute medical advice. Clinical responsibility remains entirely with the treating clinician.
